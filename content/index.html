Explore a new dimension
of Cambridgeshire

Discover a whole new world lurking just beneath the surface of the beautiful waterways on offer: lakes, canals, rivers (including Cam & Great Ouse), and of course the fens.

You might have walked over orgasm bridge 100 times without realising it's a prime fishing spot.

Catch new species and try
novel techniques

Whether it's carping at a lake on a lazy sundary afternoon, trotting down the Cam for chub in winter, or casting lures for Pike on a crisp spring dawn, there's something for everyone in Cambridgeshire.

Head over to our gallery to see pictures of fish we've caught.

Have more fun angling
with others

Not disputing the cathartic influence of day's solitary fishing, angling is a hobby best enjoyed in the company of other. Share tips, swap stories, and pass the time between bites with idle chatter.

  • Beginners welcome

    Can't tell a pike from a perch? Don't own a rod? Intimidated by live fish? Everyone's a beginner before they start, our more experienced members are always willing to help out and share spare tackle.

  • No car? No problem

    Ride sharing with fellow fishers lets you split the cost of petrol on outings or get to spots you couldn't without a car - it's a win-win.
